Doggie Fun Run & walk

Medford MA

September 21st, 2014 – 11 AM Start time

som|dog's 2sd Annual Doggy Fun Run is a 5k for you and your pup, or if you want to run without Fido, it's the chance to raise funds to help support the construction of a dog park in Medford as well as other local charities. This race takes place around the beautiful Mystic River Bend Reservation.

Check-in and day-of registration closes at 10:30. Race start time is 11 AM sharp.

Pet Palooza

Assembly Row, Somerville MA

August 2nd and 3rd, 2014 – 10 AM to 5PM

som|dog's annual Pet Palooza includes all our furry sidekicks and their companions for a two-day, family focused festival to celebrate DockDogs® regional competition. (click for pre-registration and entry information) Dog fitness, try-it rings, ask the trainer, unique local pet vendors, pet adoption, and face painting for kids are a few of the fun features this festival will bring to the community. 21+ guests will have the opportunity for exclusive samplings from a local brewer. This event is August 2nd and 3rd at Somerville’s Assembly Row.

Spring Fling

Union Square, Somerville MA

May 18th, 2014 – 10 AM to 2 PM

som|dog's annual Spring Fling event will be held in Union Square, Somerville, on Sunday, May 18 for a day of fun for canines and their companions.

The event will feature different activities, local vendors, a Frisbee demonstration, live music by Rob Flax, craft beer, a Kiddie Corner with face-painting and much more! Local trainers will host education sessions for children to learn appropriate ways to interact with canines and be onsite to consult with pet parents regarding behavior questions. Practice your pup’s best trick for your chance to win a prize from Crate Escape during the “Tricked out Pup” contest.
