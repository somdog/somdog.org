som|dog Events

Pet Palooza

Assembly Row, Somerville MA

August 1st and 2nd, 2015 – 10 AM to 5 PM

som|dog's annual Pet Palooza will kick off this with a TechBark launch party, Friday July 31st from 6–10 as we bring dogs, beer and technology together for a bark tech social.

The festivities begin on Saturday, August 1st to include all our furry sidekicks and their companions for a two-day, family focused festival to celebrate dog aquatics, and to get our community of dog owners in the pool (write to info@somdog.org for pre-registration information). The event will have test-it-out rings, ask the trainer, unique local pet vendors, a day focused on per adoption (Sunday), music, and face painting for kids are a few of the fun features this festival will bring to the community. 21+ guests will have the opportunity to relax in our beer garden. This event is August 1st and 2nd at Somerville’s Assembly Row.

Tom Taylor Memorial 5k Fun Run & Walk With Dogs

Somerville MA

November 1th, 2015 – 11 AM Start time

som|dog's Tom Taylor Memorial Doggie 5k and Fun Run is a community event for you and your pup! It's a chance to support our local dogs and raise funds in support of off-leash recreation. The race will take you on an exciting course in and out of Somerville’s Union Square.

Check-in and day-of registration closing time is 10:30 and the race begins at 11 AM sharp.

Spring Fling

Union Square, Somerville MA

May 18th, 2014 – 10 AM to 2 PM

som|dog's annual Spring Fling event will be held in Union Square, Somerville, on Sunday, May 18 for a day of fun for canines and their companions.

The event will feature different activities, local vendors, a Frisbee demonstration, live music by Rob Flax, craft beer, a Kiddie Corner with face-painting and much more! Local trainers will host education sessions for children to learn appropriate ways to interact with canines and be onsite to consult with pet parents regarding behavior questions. Practice your pup’s best trick for your chance to win a prize from Crate Escape during the “Tricked out Pup” contest.
